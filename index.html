DOT Age Checker

Where to find it?

Look for the letters "DOT" followed by a series of characters. The last 4 digits (often in a raised oval) represent the week and year of manufacture.

TireBuddy Safety Note

⚠️ Important: This check is for age only. Even if your tire is young, always check your tread depth. If the tread is worn down to the wear bars, replace your tire immediately regardless of its age!
